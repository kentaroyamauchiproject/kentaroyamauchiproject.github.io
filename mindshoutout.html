MindShoutOut

Available on the App Store — free

MindShoutOut is an early-phase loop scheduling tool for a thought that is too heavy to carry in the mind.

It gives persistent thoughts a future moment so they no longer require continuous attention.

What It Is

Not a productivity system. Not a task manager.

A structured method for scheduling or storing a single thought.

Common Uses

  • Write down a thought that is persistent "too important to forget" and schedule it to return later as notification.
  • Record an "Audio Shout" with an editable label.
  • Set a reminder with optional Acknowledgement on delivery.
  • Store a thought without scheduling to remove it from active memory.
  • Send a thought ("shout") or Audio Shout's label to MindZoneOut for soft and fuzzy "whisper" display on the blank wall.

Used when a heavy thought (that is too important to forget) repeatedly interrupts focus.

Version 1.3

  • Acknowledgement mode on delivery
  • Editable "Audio Shout" labels
  • Ability to turn Notification on and off per shout (thought)
