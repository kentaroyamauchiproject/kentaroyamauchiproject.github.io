Don't "solve" thoughts, just "place" them.

MINDBEBOP utilizes the brain's biological mechanisms to create a "toolkit" for mind brushing — allowing persistent thought loops to finally reach a closing point.

Notice unnecessary thought friction in the moment, before it occupies your "now."

A completely new approach: Thought care at the very moment of origin.
Based on the concept of metacognition ("Mind Noticing"), this Mind-Brushing Kit is a set of ultralight apps. They use "glitches" in the brain to brush off residual thoughts and restore mental silence.

No guidance, no statistics, no intrusive notifications.
Just the reduction of thought friction. Nothing else.

A toothbrush for the mind

We wash our bodies and brush our teeth every day. But what about the "mental dirt" that accumulates throughout the day?

Endless rumination, anxiety about the future, the pressure of our roles. These "residual thoughts" stick to the brain and continuously drain our energy.

MINDBEBOP is not a "therapy" to radically change your life. It's about giving accumulated thoughts a proper "landing spot" and brushing off the day's burden. A simple routine for mental hygiene to return to your natural, clear consciousness.

The Apps — Your Mind-Brushing Kit

Each app reduces the friction of unnecessary thoughts competing for your attention.

MindFlipOut

Size: approx. 1MB
App Store (Free)

Stop mental idling. A full stop for looping thoughts.

The brain repeats unfinished thoughts (Zeigarnik Effect). Catch the thought, flip the perspective (**flip**), and give a one-time response. This signals "done" to the brain and abruptly breaks the loop.


MindShoutOut

Size: approx. 1.3MB
App Store (Free)

The "Thought Cloakroom." Shed the burden through externalization.

The pressure of not forgetting creates enormous friction. By delegating thoughts outward (**shout**), your brain gains the certainty: "I don't need to worry about this now." This creates immediate mental space.


MindZoneOut

Size: approx. 1.2MB
App Store (Free)

Digital Zen. A tool for "Information Offline" mode.

Our brain constantly tries to interpret information. MindZoneOut provides a visual "wall" to simply switch off (**zone out**). Without the obligation to understand, thought activity cools down autonomously.


MindBackOut

Temporarily log out of "Self." A "Mental Backstage" to shed roles.

Whether parent, employee, or responsible adult — roles exhaust us. MindBackOut is a sanctuary to briefly step out of social expectations (**back out**). A space without self-optimization, just for pure being.


MindEaseOut

A gentle "Return to Now." Immediate relief from thought friction.

Regret about the past or worry about the future cannot be controlled in the now. MindEaseOut anchors you in physical reality through the rhythm of time and breath (grounding). It gently leads you (**ease out**) from fictional worlds of worry back into the only reality: this moment.

MindEaseOut is integrated as a core function in all MINDBEBOP apps.
