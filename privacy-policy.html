MINDBEBOP Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-02-22

1. Introduction

MINDBEBOP (“we”, “our”, “the developer”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all MINDBEBOP applications, including MindFlipOut, MindShoutOut, MindZoneOut, and any built-in modes or features released under the MINDBEBOP brand, including time-based thought release functionality.

All MINDBEBOP apps operate entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect or transmit any of the following data:

  • Personal information
  • Email address or contact information
  • Location data
  • Identifiers (device ID, advertising ID)
  • Analytics, usage data, diagnostics, or crash logs
  • Contacts, photos, files, or browsing history
  • Information from third-party SDKs

MINDBEBOP apps do not use external servers, cloud storage, or third-party analytics services. All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Data You Create

Depending on the app, you may create text entries, written thoughts, responses, reminders, scheduled notifications, audio recordings, or session-related content.

All such data:

  • Remains stored only on your device
  • Is not transmitted to MINDBEBOP
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Notifications

Some apps use local notifications to deliver reminders or messages related to thoughts you have chosen to set aside. Notification content is created, stored, and delivered entirely on your device.

5. Audio Features

If an app allows audio recording or playback:

  • Audio recordings remain stored only on your device
  • No recordings are uploaded, transmitted, or analyzed externally
  • You may delete audio at any time

6. Inter-App Communication

Some MINDBEBOP apps can optionally communicate with each other using on-device iOS mechanisms (such as custom URL schemes, local system features, or shared on-device storage).

This may include:

  • Sending a thought from MindFlipOut to MindShoutOut
  • Sending text content from MindShoutOut to MindZoneOut for on-device display (including Shouts and related labels)
  • Sending reflection text from MindZoneOut to MindFlipOut when using Manual Flip or Auto Flip
  • Sending text from MindBackOut to MindShoutOut when using the “Leave at the Door” feature
  • Opening MindZoneOut automatically from MindBackOut when Zone Cooldown is enabled

All such communication:

  • Occurs entirely on your device
  • Does not use internet servers
  • Does not transmit data outside your device
  • Shares only the minimum content required to perform the requested action

7. No Third-Party Access

MINDBEBOP apps do not use:

  • Analytics frameworks
  • Advertising networks
  • Tracking technologies
  • External data services

8. Security

Because MINDBEBOP apps do not collect or transmit data, security is handled by your device and Apple’s iOS protections, including encrypted local storage, passcode protection, Touch ID, and Face ID where available.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

App-Specific Policies

MindFlipOut

MindFlipOut is a local journaling and thought-response app. All written thoughts, rewrites, and reflections remain stored only on your device.

If Manual Shout or Auto Shout features are enabled and MindShoutOut is installed, the text of a thought response may be shared locally with MindShoutOut. This sharing occurs only on your device and never leaves it.

MindShoutOut

MindShoutOut stores reminder text, scheduled times, optional speech settings, and audio recordings locally on your device. Notifications are generated using iOS local notification services only.

If Manual Zone or Auto Zone features are enabled and MindZoneOut is installed, the text of a reminder related to a thought may be shared locally with MindZoneOut for on-device display.

MindZoneOut

MindZoneOut is a local stillness and reflection app. Incoming messages, short reflections, and session-related content are processed and displayed entirely on your device.

If Manual Flip or Auto Flip features are used and MindFlipOut is installed, reflection text may be shared locally with MindFlipOut to continue working with the thought. No data is transmitted outside your device.

Time-Based Thought Release (MindEaseOut function)

All MINDBEBOP apps include a time-based thought release feature that allows a thought to be acknowledged as relating to the past or future.

When used, text entered into this feature:

  • Is processed entirely on-device
  • Is not stored as a history unless explicitly saved by the user
  • Is not transmitted to other apps or external services
  • Is not collected by MINDBEBOP

MindBackOut

MindBackOut is a local session-based app that temporarily blocks selected applications using Apple’s Screen Time system. All configuration and session data are stored entirely on your device.

When you select apps to leave outside, this information is stored locally and used only by Apple’s on-device Screen Time framework to apply temporary restrictions. MINDBEBOP does not receive or access this information.

If you enter text in the “Leave at the Door” field and choose to send it to MindShoutOut, the text is transferred locally between apps using on-device iOS mechanisms. This transfer does not use external servers and does not leave your device.

If the optional “Zone Cooldown” feature is enabled, MindBackOut may automatically open MindZoneOut after a session ends. No data is transmitted externally during this process. The interaction occurs entirely on your device.

MindBackOut does not collect:

  • App usage history
  • Screen Time statistics
  • Device activity reports
  • Blocked app names outside your device
  • Any personal or identifying information
