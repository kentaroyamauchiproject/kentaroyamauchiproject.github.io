Press & Background

MINDBEBOP is the Mental Background OS — an independent, founder-led system of ultralight apps designed to reduce unnecessary mental background activity.

Rather than guiding behavior, tracking habits, or offering therapy, MINDBEBOP focuses on what runs quietly behind attention: looping thoughts, unfinished reminders, overstimulation, and identity-based pressure.

Each module reduces a different form of background activity — allowing attention to move freely again.

“MINDBEBOP is not therapy or productivity. It is system maintenance for the mind.”

About MINDBEBOP

MINDBEBOP defines the first modular system for regulating the Mental Background layer.

Just as an operating system manages background processes on a device, MINDBEBOP reduces unnecessary mental background activity — without analysis, coaching, or self-optimization.

It is intentionally non-clinical and non-therapeutic. The goal is not improvement or treatment, but reduction of friction.

What MINDBEBOP is — and is not

MINDBEBOP is:

  • A modular system for regulating the Mental Background
  • Reduction of unnecessary background activity
  • Ultralight, on-device modules
  • Non-performative and pressure-free
  • Designed to disengage quickly

MINDBEBOP is not:

  • Therapy or medical treatment
  • A diagnostic system
  • A self-optimization or productivity program
  • A replacement for professional mental health care

Positioning

MINDBEBOP occupies a space that currently has no direct equivalent. It is the first modular system specifically designed to regulate the Mental Background layer rather than guide behavior or optimize performance. Most mind-related apps either guide behavior (journaling, mindfulness, therapy, productivity) or capture attention (media, games, ambient tools).

MINDBEBOP does not aim to guide behavior or retain attention. Its modules are designed to reduce background activity and then disengage — without goals, tracking, feeds, or habit loops.

The Apps

MindFlipOut — Reduces looping thought activity by allowing thoughts to receive a clear response.

MindShoutOut — Reduces open-loop activity by safely scheduling important thoughts to return later.

MindZoneOut — Reduces stimulation activity through intentional visual stillness.

MindEaseOut — A built-in release function that helps completed thoughts disengage from the present.

MindBackOut — Reduces role-based pressure by temporarily restricting access to identity-linked apps.

Founder

Kentaro Yamauchi — Founder / Creator
Kentaro Yamauchi created MINDBEBOP from lived experience and observation rather than from a clinical or academic framework. His work focuses on designing simple, humane tools that support everyday mental hygiene without turning inner life into a performance or diagnosis.

The project grew out of everyday habits — using a notebook and plain text tools to write thoughts down, save them, and move on. Over time, those small practices became the foundation for tools designed to help thoughts land, be acknowledged, and released before becoming residue.

Privacy & Data

MINDBEBOP apps operate entirely on the user’s device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

Anything created in the apps stays on the device and is removed when the app is uninstalled.
