Refusals

What the MINDBEBOP system deliberately refuses to do.

MINDBEBOP is defined as much by what it leaves out as by what it includes.

These are not missing features. They are boundaries — placed on the system, not on the person using it.

  • No goals, targets, or outcomes The system does not push you toward improvement.
  • No progress tracking, streaks, or scores Nothing accumulates. Nothing compounds.
  • No evaluation of thoughts, emotions, or usage Nothing is judged, ranked, or interpreted.
  • No coaching, instruction, or optimization The system does not tell you what to do next.
  • No identity shaping or self-improvement framing You are not being trained into a better version.
  • No emotional manipulation or reward loops Nothing is designed to hook or retain you.
  • No pressure to return, continue, or complete Using it once is enough.
  • No large file size — around 1 MB per app Quick to open. Easy to keep. Easy to leave.
  • No cloud sync Thoughts are handled locally and then allowed to end.
  • No tracking or analytics Nothing is measured in the background.
  • No accounts or sign-ins Nothing to maintain or manage.
  • No reminders to come back No nudges, check-ins, or prompts created by the system itself.

MINDBEBOP does not try to keep you engaged.
It exists to help thoughts end — and then get out of the way.
